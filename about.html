SERVICES

WE PROVIDE INDUSTRY TREND SERVICES TO OUR CLIENTS GLOBALLY WITH THE FOLLOWING

***
Antivirus Softwares

Our computer programs used to prevent, detect, and remove malware are among the world's best. Antivirus softwares will be tailored to detect and remove computer viruses in your organisation.

***
Vulnerability Analysis

We conduct in-depth analysis of your systems, to identify weaknesses and lack of redundancy, and determine mitigations or corrective actions that can be designed or implemented to reduce the cyberattack

***
Penetration Testing

Our professional Ethical hackers perform authorized simulated cyberattack on your computer systems, to evaluate the security of the system using similar attacks used by the bad guys or hackers.

***
Reverse Engineering

When there is an attacked or suspected attack on your systems, we evaluate the attack to understand the source, damage and possible unintended actions by your organisation which led to the attack.
